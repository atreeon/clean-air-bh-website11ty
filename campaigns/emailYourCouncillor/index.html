Email Your Councillor #

To email your councillors please select your ward from the dropdown menu below. You can then send an email councillors you represent you, in your own short words, to support clean air initiatives in Brighton & Hove. It is best to include your name and address so that they know you are a constituent.

If you are unsure of your ward, you can find out here

https://www.brighton-hove.gov.uk/council-and-democracy/councillor-ward-map
